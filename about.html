Plenary Consulting, established in April 2021, is a small information technology consulting company providing small food service businesses (such as bakeries, coffee shops, or restaurants) in the San Antonio, TX area with a competitive advantage through proven information technology solutions at an affordable price.

Asif Fasih thought of the idea in January 2021 and drew from his 12 years of professional experience working as a Software Engineer for a Fortune 100 company. Asif decided to help small food service businesses with their technology needs because of the impact of the COVID-19 pandemic.

We invite you to learn more about our services or contact us for a free initial assessment!
