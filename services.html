Plenary Consulting offers two services:

  1. Consulting Project
  2. Ongoing and Hourly Technical Support

Consulting Project
This includes:

  • A free initial assessment to understand if the business has a physical location or mobile location (for example, a food truck).
  • Evaluating existing hardware and software at your business (estimate: 2 weeks)
  • Researching options for hardware and software (estimate: 2 to 4 weeks)
  • Implementation (estimate: 6 to 10 weeks)
  • Initial Support (1 week)

Ongoing and Hourly Technical Support
This includes:

  • Contracts for ongoing support focuses on providing support for solutions implemented or evaluated by Plenary Consulting.
    • We will perform maintenance on hardware, software, and the businesses' website periodically.
    • We will update digital menus regularly. To ensure the client stays current, we will also upgrade hardware and software on a regular cadence.
    • We will provide training to all employees on hardware and software solutions, including working with digital menus from a training perspective.
  • Hourly technical support focuses on non-routine needs.
    • These are typically related to solving a problem that is either an emergency or non-emergency.
    • We will troubleshoot Wi-Fi, network, router issues and why computers, printers, or tablets are frozen.
    • We will remove any computer viruses, spyware, and malware.
    • We will troubleshoot any other hardware and software failures not mentioned previously.
